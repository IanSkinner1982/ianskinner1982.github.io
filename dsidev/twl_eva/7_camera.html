--- layout: devinfo app: TWL_EVA_CAMERA altname: CAMERA category: [TWL EVA] sequence: TRUE redirect_from: /dsidev/twl_eva_camera.html group: true --- CAMERA is used to test the DSi cameras. These tests allow for changing exposure, lighting, sharpness, size, filters, etc. The extended camera settings are not available with any other software and do not seem to actually calibrate the DSi. Instead they just appear to be for viewing errors under different conditions. Taking a photo CAMERA will allow you to view it in 9 zoomed in sections to check for fine errors.
