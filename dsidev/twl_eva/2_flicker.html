--- layout: devinfo app: TWL_EVA_FLICKER altname: FLICKER category: [TWL EVA] sequence: TRUE redirect_from: /dsidev/twl_eva_flicker.html group: true --- FLICKER is a DSi screen tester. There are two parts to this test, FLICKER ADJUSTER and LCD UNIT CHECKER. The first one displays lines close together that will cause flickering if the screens are not calibrated, and the second will display colors and patterns. You can see the flickering on your own screen by zooming in or out on the second screenshot below.
