SDMC Launcher DSi

A retail DSi from the factory that is still running the unique SDMC Launcher bootloader. Thanks to BrolyBroMan for sharing!


X6 DSi and factory firmwares

An X6 prototype DSi and 3 final retail motherboards with factory (prototype) firmware. Huge thanks to MarioDS64 for sharing his prototype, HudsonU for his factory firmware, and Kelly for helping me get ahold of my two boards.


Classroom + MangaON DSi dev NANDs

8 NAND dumps from various DSi development units (and 2 retail), some of which were used for developing the Classroom DSi and MangaON. There is also a development cartridge containing the KART, a possible augmented reality test.
Many thanks to collection_nintendo on Instagram for providing the dumps.


X4 Prototype released

Lots of detailed photos and the differences of a newly discovered X4 prototype DS lite. Huge thanks to lasscat and ProtonX!
