--- layout: devinfo app: NANDSoundPlayer redirect_from: /dsidev/soundplayer.html preview: /preview.gif --- NAND SoundPlayer plays music/sound effects a .sdat sound archive on the SD card. You can play sounds at either 32730 or 47610 Hz, and use different sound effects (stereo, mono, surround, etc).

The app also shows a status of all the voice channels. The playing of multiple sounds at once is supported.
